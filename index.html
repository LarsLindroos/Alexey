His Greatest Achievements

“There’s nothing extraordinary in what I did. The fact that I’ve been turned into a legend irritates me.” -Alexey Petrovich Maresyev (during an interview)

Quick Points on Alexey Maresyev's Achievements:

• The Story of a Real Man was written by Sergei Prokofiev from 1947 to 1948 and was his last opera.

• The libretto, by the composer and Mira Mendelson, is based on the novel of the same name by Boris Polevoy; this in turn was based on the story of pilot Alexey Maresyev.

• The story is set during the Second World War.

• In 1943, he won the hero of the Soviet Union Award

• This was, at the time, the highest military decoration in the USSR

• He flew 86 combat missions

• He eventually mastered the use of his prosthetics

• Later joined the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet parliament, and pushed for the rights of disabled war veterans
